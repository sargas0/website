FAQ

How much lead time do alerts provide?

Lead time varies with distance, currents, and wind; in many cases you’ll receive days-ahead notice with updates as conditions change.

What communication channels do you support?

Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and webhook. We can discuss other integrations during onboarding.

How long does onboarding take?

Typical onboarding completes in about two weeks, including beach segmentation and alert thresholds.

Do you support multiple beaches?

Yes. We segment each shoreline and manage alerts per location and threshold.
